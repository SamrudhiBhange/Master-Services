System Integration


        

A systems integrator (SI) is an entity that specializes in bringing together component subsystems into a whole and ensuring that those subsystems work together towards the functional needs of the organization. Systems integrators may work in many fields but the term is generally used in the information technology (IT). A typical SI is involved from pre-transaction consulting to the delivery of integrated product/service to post transaction consulting.

We can bring together best of the breed Technology from Global Leaders like Dell, Microsoft, Oracle, VMware, Cisco, IBM, Redhat, Symantec etc to create a state of the art IT set up like Data Center, Intelligent Network, e-office, Secured Gateways and Networks. We can procure, integrate and manage the entire gamut of products and services like Desktops/Laptops, Servers, Firewalls, Backup Solutions, LAN, WAN, System and Network Security, End Point Management etc.
